Soft washing

Soft Washing Orlando FL

Low-pressure house washing for siding, stucco, and painted exteriors — safer than high-pressure blasting on fragile surfaces.

House washing without surface damage

High pressure can etch siding, force water behind trim, and strip paint. Soft washing uses lower pressure with appropriate detergents to kill organic growth and rinse clean.

  • Vinyl & painted siding
  • Stucco & dryvit
  • Brick & mortar (careful pressure only)
  • Soffits, fascia & gutters
  • Outdoor living areas

Pressure washing can damage older brick, mortar, painted surfaces, glass, and electrical components when used incorrectly. Match method to material.
