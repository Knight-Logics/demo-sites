How We Work

Our Installation Process

We follow a proven, transparent five-step process to ensure every installation is completed on time, on budget, and to the highest standard.

1

Initial Consultation

We begin with a thorough assessment of your property, discussing your needs, preferences, and budget. Our experts provide recommendations tailored to your unique situation.

2

Customized Plan

Based on the consultation, we develop a detailed installation plan that outlines the materials, timeline, and cost — giving you a clear picture before any work starts.

3

Professional Installation

Our skilled team executes the installation with precision and care, using best-in-class materials and techniques to ensure a flawless, long-lasting result.

4

Final Inspection

Once installation is complete, we conduct a comprehensive inspection to verify that every aspect of the roof meets our rigorous quality standards and your expectations.

5

Post-Installation Support

Our relationship doesn't end at installation. We provide ongoing support, maintenance advice, and our industry-leading 15-year workmanship warranty for complete peace of mind.
