Enhance Your Home With Natural Light

Enhance your home with natural light through our skylight installation and repair services. We ensure proper installation and maintenance, adding beauty and functionality to your living spaces while maintaining the watertight integrity of your roof.

A properly installed skylight transforms dark rooms, reduces electricity costs, and adds lasting value to your property. Our team handles every detail — from structural framing to flashing and waterproofing — so your skylight performs beautifully for decades.

Benefits Of Skylight Services

Natural Light & Ambiance — Skylights fill your home with warm, natural daylight, creating a brighter, more inviting living environment.

Energy Savings — Natural lighting reduces your reliance on artificial lighting during the day, lowering your energy bills.

Increased Home Value — Skylights are a desirable feature that adds aesthetic appeal and market value to your property.

Ventilation Options — Vented skylights can be opened to improve airflow and reduce heat buildup in Florida's warm climate.

Proper Waterproofing — Our expert installation ensures a completely watertight seal — no leaks, no condensation problems.

Why Trust Roof Monsters With Your Skylight?

If your existing skylight is leaking, fogging between panes, or has damaged flashing, our specialists diagnose the problem accurately and perform precise repairs to restore full function without a full replacement.

Our Approach

The Skylight Installation Process

We follow a meticulous process to ensure every skylight we install delivers lasting performance, proper waterproofing, and a beautiful finish.

1

Consultation & Selection

We discuss your goals, preferred placement, size, and style. We recommend the right skylight type — fixed, vented, or tubular — based on your home's structure and your needs.

2

Structural Assessment

Before cutting, our team evaluates your roof structure and attic space to plan the installation — ensuring proper framing support and minimal impact on your home's integrity.

3

Professional Installation

Our certified roofers install the skylight with precision, using premium flashing, sealants, and waterproof membranes to create a completely weathertight installation.

4

Interior Finishing

We complete the installation with clean, professional interior finishing — including drywall, trim, or a light shaft as needed — so the result looks beautiful from every angle.

5

Final Inspection

We conduct a thorough post-installation inspection and water test to verify the seal is perfect before we leave. Your satisfaction is guaranteed before we sign off on any job.
