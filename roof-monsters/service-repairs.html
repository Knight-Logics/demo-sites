What We Fix

Our Repair Services

We handle every type of roofing issue — from small patches to complete structural restoration.

Leak Detection & Repair

Our advanced diagnostic tools pinpoint leaks quickly and accurately. We seal every entry point to stop water intrusion and prevent future damage.

Shingle Replacement

Cracked, missing, or curling shingles compromise your roof's protection. We replace damaged shingles with matching materials for a seamless, durable repair.

Flashing Repair

Faulty flashing around chimneys, vents, and skylights is a leading cause of leaks. We reseal and replace flashing to restore a watertight barrier.

Preventive Maintenance

Our scheduled maintenance programs identify and address small issues before they become major problems, saving you money and extending your roof's life.

Structural Repairs

When damage goes below the shingles, our team repairs or replaces decking, rafters, and other structural components to restore full integrity.
