## Important verification gaps before a real launch

- Exact legal business name and entity structure
- Primary business city and real service radius
- Mailing address or office address
- Insurance documentation details behind the "fully insured" claim
- Exact experience claim if it should be 10+ years or higher
- Warranty structure and workmanship guarantee language
- Material brands and roof systems offered
- Public review sources and testimonial permissions
- Whether the Facebook presence should stay personal-profile based or move to a proper business page
